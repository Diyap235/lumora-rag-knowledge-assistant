Space-O Technologies — Company Overview
========================================

About Space-O Technologies
---------------------------
Space-O Technologies is an AI-powered software development company founded in 2010.
Headquarters: Ahmedabad, India.
The company builds AI agents, copilots, and intelligent software solutions that drive business growth.
They serve clients across North America, Europe, Australia, and the Middle East.
Space-O has delivered over 300 mobile and web applications.

Founders and Leadership
-----------------------
Space-O Technologies was co-founded by Nivan Shah and Atit Shah.
The leadership team drives the company's vision of building AI and software solutions that solve real business problems.

Services
--------
- Custom AI Development: AI agents, copilots, LLM integrations, RAG-based systems
- Mobile App Development: iOS and Android apps
- Web Application Development: Full-stack custom web platforms
- Software Product Development: End-to-end product engineering
- UI/UX Design: User experience design and prototyping
- Quality Assurance: Manual and automated testing

Technology Stack
----------------
- AI/ML: OpenAI, LangChain, LlamaIndex, Hugging Face, TensorFlow, PyTorch
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter, Swift, Kotlin
- Web: React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, Django, FastAPI
- Cloud: AWS, Google Cloud, Azure
- Databases: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, ChromaDB, Pinecone
