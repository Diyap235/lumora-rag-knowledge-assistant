SECTION 1: LEAVE OVERVIEW
================================================================

What is the leave policy at Space-O Technologies?
Space-O Technologies provides all full-time employees with a structured leave policy to ensure work-life balance while maintaining business continuity. Employees are entitled to various types of leaves including Earned Leave, Sick Leave, Casual Leave, Maternity Leave, Paternity Leave, and Compensatory Off.

How many total leave days do employees get per year?
Full-time employees at Space-O Technologies are entitled to the following leaves per calendar year:
- Earned Leave (EL): 15 days per year
- Sick Leave (SL): 7 days per year
- Casual Leave (CL): 7 days per year
- Public Holidays: 12 days per year (as per company calendar)
Total: 29 days of paid leave per year excluding public holidays.

When does leave balance reset?
Leave balances reset on January 1st of every calendar year. New employees get leave on a pro-rata basis from their date of joining.

How is leave balance calculated for new employees?
For employees joining mid-year, leave is calculated on a pro-rata basis. For example, if you join in July, you are entitled to 50% of the annual leave quota for that calendar year.


SECTION 2: TYPES OF LEAVE
================================================================

What is Earned Leave (EL)?
Earned Leave is paid leave that employees earn by working. Employees earn 1.25 days of EL per month, totaling 15 days per year. EL must be applied for at least 7 days in advance for planned leaves.

What is Sick Leave (SL)?
Sick Leave is paid leave provided when an employee is unwell and unable to attend work. Employees get 7 days of Sick Leave per year. For sick leave of more than 2 consecutive days, a medical certificate from a registered doctor is required.

What is Casual Leave (CL)?
Casual Leave is for personal or urgent matters that require an employee to be away from work. Employees get 7 days of Casual Leave per year. Casual leave can be taken for half days as well.

What are public holidays?
Space-O Technologies observes 12 public holidays per year as declared in the annual company holiday calendar. The calendar is shared at the beginning of each year.

What is Compensatory Off (Comp Off)?
If an employee works on a declared public holiday or weekend due to project requirements, they are entitled to a compensatory off day. Comp Off must be applied within 30 days of the working holiday.


SECTION 3: HOW TO APPLY FOR LEAVE
================================================================

What is the process to apply for leave?
The process to apply for leave at Space-O Technologies is as follows:
Step 1: Log in to the HR portal (HRMS system).
Step 2: Navigate to Leave Management section.
Step 3: Select the type of leave (EL, SL, CL).
Step 4: Enter the start date and end date.
Step 5: Add a reason for the leave.
Step 6: Submit the leave application.
Step 7: Your reporting manager will receive a notification and approve or reject the leave.
Step 8: You will receive an email confirmation once the leave is approved.

How do I apply for leave?
To apply for leave, log in to the HRMS portal, go to Leave Management, select the leave type, enter your dates, add a reason, and submit. Your manager will approve or reject the request and you will be notified by email.

What are the steps to request time off?
To request time off: login to HRMS → Leave Management → Apply Leave → select type → enter dates → add reason → submit. Manager approval is required. Apply at least 7 days in advance for planned leaves.

How much notice is required before taking leave?
- Earned Leave (Planned): Minimum 7 days advance notice required.
- Casual Leave: Minimum 1 day advance notice required.
- Sick Leave: Inform your reporting manager by 10:00 AM on the day of absence via phone, WhatsApp, or email.
- Emergency Leave: Inform your reporting manager immediately by phone call.

Can I apply for half-day leave?
Yes, employees can apply for half-day leave for Casual Leave and Earned Leave. Half-day leave counts as 0.5 days from your leave balance. Half days are either morning (9:00 AM to 1:00 PM) or afternoon (2:00 PM to 5:45 PM).


SECTION 4: LONG LEAVE AND EXTENDED ABSENCE
================================================================

How do I apply for a long leave of more than 30 days?
For leaves between 31 and 90 consecutive days, the following process applies:
- Submit the leave application at least 30 days in advance.
- Attach a detailed handover plan covering all ongoing projects.
- Attach supporting documentation (medical, personal reasons, etc.).
- Approval required from: Reporting Manager + HRBP + HR Head + Delivery VP.
- The leave request will be reviewed within 5 working days.
- Medical leave of 31 or more days requires a doctor's certificate every 15 days.

What happens if I need more than 90 days of leave?
For leaves exceeding 90 consecutive days, the employee must:
- Apply at least 45 days in advance.
- Submit documentation to HR Head directly.
- Approval required from: HR Head + CEO.
- A formal leave of absence agreement will be drafted.
- Salary and benefits during this period will be discussed case-by-case.

What is the maximum continuous leave I can take?
Without special approval, the maximum continuous leave is 30 days. Leaves between 31-90 days require HRBP and HR Head approval. Leaves exceeding 90 days require CEO approval.


SECTION 5: LEAVE WITHOUT ALLOCATED BALANCE
================================================================

What happens if I don't have enough leave days left?
If your leave balance is insufficient, you have three options:
Option 1 — Leave Without Pay (LWP): You can take unpaid leave. Your salary will be deducted proportionally for the days taken. Apply through the HRMS portal the same way as regular leave and select LWP as the type.
Option 2 — Advance Leave: With manager and HR approval, you can borrow up to 5 days from next year's leave quota. This will be adjusted from next year's balance.
Option 3 — Compensatory Off: If you have accumulated Comp Off days, those can be used instead.

What is Leave Without Pay (LWP)?
Leave Without Pay means you take a leave day but your salary is deducted for that day. The deduction is calculated as: Monthly Salary ÷ 26 working days × Number of LWP days. LWP requires manager approval and should be used only when no other leave balance is available.

Can I take leave if my balance is zero?
Yes, but it will be counted as Leave Without Pay (LWP). Your salary will be deducted for those days. You must still apply through the HRMS portal and get manager approval. You cannot simply be absent without applying.

Can I borrow leaves from next year?
Yes, advance leave of up to 5 days can be borrowed from the next year's quota with manager and HR approval. This is granted only in genuine cases and will be deducted from the following year's leave balance.


SECTION 6: IF LEAVE IS NOT APPROVED
================================================================

What should I do if my leave is not approved?
If your leave application is rejected by your manager, you should:
Step 1: Speak directly with your reporting manager to understand the reason for rejection.
Step 2: If the reason is workload or project deadline, discuss alternate dates that work for both parties.
Step 3: If you believe the rejection is unjustified, escalate to your HRBP (HR Business Partner).
Step 4: If the issue remains unresolved, contact the HR Head.
Space-O has a fair leave policy and genuine leave requests are always considered.

What happens if my leave request is rejected?
If your leave is rejected, you will receive a notification in the HRMS portal with the reason. You can reapply for different dates, discuss with your manager, or escalate to HR if the rejection seems unfair. You should NOT be absent without approval.

Can I escalate a rejected leave to HR?
Yes. If you feel your leave was unfairly rejected, you can raise it with your HRBP or HR Head. Provide the leave details, the reason given by your manager, and your justification. HR will mediate and help find a resolution.

What if I go on leave without approval?
Absence without approved leave is treated as Leave Without Pay and may result in a disciplinary warning. Repeated unauthorized absences can lead to further disciplinary action as per the company's Code of Conduct.


SECTION 7: CARRY FORWARD AND ENCASHMENT
================================================================

Can I carry forward unused leave to next year?
Yes. Up to 15 days of Earned Leave can be carried forward to the next calendar year. Sick Leave and Casual Leave cannot be carried forward and will lapse at year end.

What is the maximum leave I can accumulate?
The maximum accumulated Earned Leave balance is 45 days (current year + carried forward). Once the balance reaches 45 days, no further accumulation happens until the balance reduces.

Can I encash my unused leave?
Leave encashment is available at the time of resignation or retirement. Earned Leave balance up to 30 days can be encashed at the time of full and final settlement. The encashment amount is calculated as: (Basic Salary ÷ 26) × Number of EL days.


SECTION 8: SPECIAL LEAVE
================================================================

What is the maternity leave policy?
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks (182 days) of paid maternity leave as per the Maternity Benefit Act, 2017. This applies to employees who have worked at Space-O for at least 80 days in the 12 months prior to the expected delivery date. Apply at least 8 weeks before the expected delivery date through the HRMS portal with a doctor's certificate.

What is the paternity leave policy?
Male employees are entitled to 5 days of paid paternity leave to be taken within 30 days of the child's birth or adoption. Apply through the HRMS portal with relevant documentation.

What leave is available for bereavement?
In case of death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling), employees are entitled to 3 days of paid bereavement leave. For extended family, 1 day is provided. Apply through HRMS and inform your manager immediately.

What is the leave policy for marriage?
Employees are entitled to 3 days of paid marriage leave (for their own marriage). This must be applied at least 15 days in advance through the HRMS portal.


SECTION 9: OFFICE HOURS AND ATTENDANCE
================================================================

What are the office hours at Space-O Technologies?
Office hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:45 PM. A 15-minute grace period is allowed in the morning for unforeseen delays. Lunch break is from 1:00 PM to 1:45 PM.

What is the late arrival policy?
Employees are allowed a 15-minute grace period (arriving by 9:15 AM). If an employee arrives after 9:15 AM, it is marked as late. More than 3 late arrivals in a month may result in a half-day leave deduction.

What is the work from home policy?
WFH is available based on project requirements and manager approval. It is not an entitlement but a privilege granted based on performance and business needs. Employees must be reachable during working hours while on WFH.

================================================================
